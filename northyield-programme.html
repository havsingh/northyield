The Opportunity

Why Liverpool, Birkenhead
& the surrounding areas.

The North West of England represents one of the strongest performing regions for cash-flowing property investment in the country. Prices remain accessible, rental demand is strong, and the area's transformation continues to drive long-term capital growth.

£

Purchase Prices Remain Low

Properties that would cost four or five times the price in the Home Counties are available in this region at valuations that make strong cashflow genuinely achievable from day one.

↑

Strong Rental Demand

Liverpool and Birkenhead both show consistent tenant demand across HMO, supported living and standard residential. Voids are low in well-managed properties in this area.

⬡

Regeneration & Growth

Birkenhead in particular sits in a window of regeneration ahead of the curve. Areas currently priced at a discount to Liverpool are expected to close that gap significantly over the coming years.

✦

HMO Without Article 4

Large parts of the Birkenhead and Wirral area sit outside Article 4 direction zones, meaning HMO conversions can be carried out under permitted development — removing planning risk entirely.

◈

Supported Living Potential

The region has strong provision networks for supported living — a strategy that can offer commercial-level rental income with reduced landlord management responsibilities when structured correctly.

◉

Established Local Network

We have spent months on the ground in this area building the right professional relationships. That infrastructure means deals move faster and with far fewer complications.

Is This For You?

This programme is designed
for a specific type of investor.

We work with a small number of serious investors at any one time. This is intentional — it means every investor gets our full attention, and every deal gets the scrutiny it deserves.

  • You have a minimum of £70,000 in investable capital

    This is the minimum required to participate in the types of deals we source in this area. Investors with more capital are welcome to discuss larger or multiple acquisitions.

  • You are cash-rich and time-poor

    You have the financial resources to invest, but your professional commitments mean you cannot dedicate the time required to source and manage a property acquisition from scratch.

  • You can make decisions quickly

    The best deals move fast. When we present an opportunity to you, we need a decision within a tight window. Investors who need several weeks to evaluate a deal will miss the best opportunities.

  • You understand that property investment carries risk

    We work hard to mitigate risk at every stage, but no investment is without it. We want investors who approach this with clarity, not unrealistic expectations.

  • You are looking for cashflow, value-add or HMO strategies

    Our expertise is in high-yielding, active strategies — not passive single-let investments. If you're looking for strong monthly income and/or equity growth, we are built for you.
